Scootchbots Info

Scootchbots are a species of autonomous agents invented by supergenius programmer Michael Trestman in 2014. They are indestructable beings of pure data that cannot be destroyed. They wander the Milky Way Galaxy, riding currents of cosmic energy, ever in search of a balance between yin and yang, love and hate, twi and laa.
Pythia of Delphi prophesied that scootchbots would eventually fill the cosmos, bringing about the end of all things, but Nicodemus of the Rosebush foretold that only scootchbots could save the Galaxy by stopping Gmork the SpaceWulf, who dwells in the supermassive black hole at the center of the Milky Way, draining the soul of night and devouring all things.

scootch bots simulator controls


p to pause and o to unpause
the green circle attracts the bots
w, s, a, d to move; c to stop.
hit m to set mouse control, then click to place

the red circle repels the bots
up, down, left, right to move; space to stop.
hit n to set mouse control, then click to place

g to turn gravity on and t to turn it off
f to turn frictison on and r to turn it off
y to turn tracing on and u to turn it off
