"Discuss a time you had an emotional conflict with someone. What was it about? What happened?"

"What was the source of the conflict?"

"I dunno. It was surprising that it turned into a big deal. she seems to have a really fragile ego and to enjoy enforcing her authority on people that she has power over because of her position (students)."

"HHow did you handle that conflict?"

"Apologized to her, or at least tried to."

"DDid your actions make the conflict better or worse?"

"Ithink at the time maybe it helped, but over time she has been a real bane of my fucking existince."

"What was the source of the conflict?"

"Her soul being a festering scab with maggots crawling out of it ."

"WIf you could go back, what would you do differently, if anything?"

"I would have called her out on her stupid bullshit then because trying to mollify her ego didn't help in the long run. Probably the best thing for my career would have been to break into her house and murder her in her sleep, but that's not really my style."
